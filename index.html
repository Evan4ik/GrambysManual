Controls

Most controls that the game tells you like Left Click (M1), Right click (M2), Q and E aren't the only controls in the game. Controls like CTRL+M1/CTRL+M2, P, or SHIFT+E or Q aren't told by the game. I'll go over each one right now.

  • M1 / Left Click - Pick up / drop item with left hand.
  • M2 / Right Click - Pick up / drop item with right hand.
  • Q - Use / activate item in left hand.
  • E - Use / activate item in right hand.
  • CTRL+M1/M2 - Pick up item without detaching it from the assembly in left or right hand.
  • P - Open up piano menu, where you can play on a keyboard with certain items.
  • SHIFT+E/Q - This opens up a settings menu for some items like radio or tripwire, and many others.
